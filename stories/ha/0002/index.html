Kalli Dabbobin Look at the animals Regardez les animaux

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Halima Lawal Daura

Hausa

Level 1

Kalli dabbodin

Look at the animals.

Regardez les animaux.

Shanu na kuka, "moo."

The cow says, "Moo."

La vache dit, « Meuh. »

Akuya na cewa, "meh, meh."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

La chèvre dit, « Bêê, bêê. »

Doki na haniniya

The horse says, "Neigh."

Le cheval dit, « Hiiii. »

Alade na gurnani

The pig says, "Grunt."

Le cochon dit, « Groin, groin. »

Kaza na yin kyarkyara

The chicken says, "Cluck."

La poule dit, « Cot-cot. »

Kare na woof-woof

The dog says, "Woof."

Le chien dit, « Wouf. »

Manomin ya ce "shhh!"

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

Le fermier dit, « Chut ! »

Written by: Jenny Katz
Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Halima Lawal Daura
Language: Hausa
Level 1
