The following languages are currently included in the scope of the Storybooks Nigeria project:

  • Nigerian Pidgin English
  • Hausa
  • Igbo
  • Yoruba
  • Zarma
  • Fulfulde / Fula / Fulani
  • Arabic
  • French
  • Atyap / Tyap / Katab
  • Birom
  • Edo
  • Efik
  • Gbagyi
  • Hyam / Jabba
  • Ibibio
  • Idoma
  • Igala
  • Igbira
  • Ijaw / Ijo
  • Ikwerre
  • Isoko
  • Itsekiri
  • Jju / Kaje
  • Jukun / Njikum / Jukun Takum
  • Kanuri
  • Margi
  • Nupe
  • Tiv
  • Urhobo
We are always looking for translators! If you speak one of the above languages and would like to help us translate stories, please contact us at globalafricanstorybook [at] gmail [dot] com
